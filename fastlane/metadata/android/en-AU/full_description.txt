BackOfficePro Stocktake lets you perform stock counts using your Android phone's camera.

Scan EAN-13 and EAN-8 barcodes. Each scan looks up the product on your BackOfficePro server (running on your local network) and lets you record the counted quantity.

Features:
- EAN-13 and EAN-8 barcode scanning via phone camera
- Create and manage stocktake sessions
- Scan products and record counted quantities
- Handles barcode aliases (multiple barcodes per product)
- Connects to BackOfficePro over your store's WiFi

No internet connection required — communicates only with your local BackOfficePro server.
No proprietary SDKs. No tracking. Open source.
